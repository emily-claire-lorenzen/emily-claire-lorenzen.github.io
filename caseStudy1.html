1025 N Broadway Collaboration Space Development

Project Overview

As part of a five-member team, I helped design and develop plans for various aspects of a new building located in the backyard of the Milwaukee School of Engineering Campus Center. This space was designed to be sustainable, accessible, and collaborative.

Problem

This project aimed to promote collaboration and connection to the natural world by utilizing currently undeveloped land in the northwest courtyard of the property at 1025 N Broadway. We were charged with designing and planning the construction of a new building and outdoor areas.

  • Instructional classroom
  • Kitchenette/garden preparation area
  • Gathering space
  • Outdoor classroom
  • Gardening space


Role & Process

On this team, I was tasked to work with my team in order to complete weekly project assignments related to many different specialty areas relating to design and construction. This specialty areas included:

  • Site Analysis and Development
  • Sustainability
  • Transportation
  • Accessibility
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Stormwater Management
  • Structural Design
  • Mechanical Systems Design
  • Electrical Systems Design
  • Life Safety Systems Design
  • Construction Management
