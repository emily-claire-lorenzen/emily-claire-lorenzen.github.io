EduLofts

Project Overview

As part of a three-member team, I helped design and test a prototype for an app to assist upperclassmen college students in finding affordable and desirable off-campus housing.

Problem

This project aimed to assist students who were interested in off-campus housing to find affordable off-campus housing. We did this by designing, prototyping, and evaluating an app that would help students search for off-campus housing, focused on filtering and note-taking features.

Role & Process

On this team, I was tasked to work with my team in order to walk through the human-centered user experience design process from problem to final prototype. This process involved:

  • Primary research via interviews
  • Secondary research
  • Research analysis via affinity diagrams
  • Personas
  • User journey maps
  • Concept sketches
  • Wireframing
  • Low-fidelity paper prototype
  • User testing and analysis
  • User flow diagrams
  • High-fidelity Figma prototype
  • Heuristic evaluations
  • Microcopy and accessibility evaluations
  • Usability testing
  • First impression testing
